Forest Growth and Number of Plantations

The Swiss Federal Statistical Office collected raw data about swiss forest growth and numbers of plantations since 1975.

The app AssignDDP94 uses data about region of east Switzerland to calculate:

  • Total Forest Area growth [ha],
  • Productive Area growth [ha]
  • Increase of Total Number of Plantations

and displays the interactive plots:

  • Forests of Canton [ha]
  • Numbers of Plantations of Canton

All R codes and data could be downloaded at github.

Input Widget

The input widget allows to choose the input parameter Canton, Type of Owner and Date Range for calculations, interactive plots and filtered data table.

Example Reactive Calculation

Output calculation of increasing Number of Plantations wihtin the choosen date range, canton and type of owner input.

output$Plantations <- renderText({
PlantationsDiff <- as.character(ForestData[which(
(ForestData$Canton == input$canton &
ForestData$TypeofOwner == input$ownerType &
ForestData$Year == (year(input$dateRange)[2])), 7]) -
(ForestData[which(ForestData$Canton == input$canton &
ForestData$TypeofOwner == input$ownerType &
ForestData$Year==(year(input$dateRange)[1])), 7])})

Data

Based on the input widget choices are the choosen filter parameter and the filtered data displayed on tab 'Table'.

  • Year: Date range from 1975 until 2016
  • Canton: Appenzell Innerrhoden, Apenzell Ausserrhoden, Glarus, Graubuenden / Grigioni / Grischun, Schaffhausen, St. Gallen and Thurgau.
  • TypeofOwner: Type of owners - Total, Private forest, Public forest. More details see about.md
